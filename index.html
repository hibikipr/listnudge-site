Shared grocery lists, with a nudge when you're at the store.

Add milk from the couch. Whoever gets to the store first checks it off — everyone else finds out instantly. Real-time sync via iCloud, no login required.

Two things that make it work

Everything else in ListNudge exists to support these two.

🛒 Collaborative

Built for the whole household

Share any list with the people you shop with. See who added what and who checked it off — items sort themselves into Produce, Dairy, Bakery, Pantry, Frozen, and Household automatically.

📍 Location-aware

Store nudges, not just reminders

ListNudge watches the stores you actually shop at and nudges you the moment you're nearby — only if your list has something on it. Quiet hours keep a late errand from buzzing your phone.
